More Tools > Territory War Squad Builder

This tool will inspect your guild's roster and allow you to build different squads for Territory War. It needs to fetch the information from swgoh.gg to do this. If your guild doesn't have everyone registered there, you'll need to fix that first.

What is your SWGOH.GG Guild Number and Name? You can find this in the URL when looking at your guild's page.
https://swgoh.gg/g//

SWGOH.GG does not allow automatic fetching of your account information, so we'll have to do it manually .

Step 1:
Click here to open your guild's collection data page on swgoh.gg

Step 2:
Copy and paste everything you see on that page into this box. Please note that this is a lot of data to paste. Your browser may stall for a few seconds. If you are using Firefox, it may try to make the data pretty. Click the "raw data" tab and copy and paste that.
